There are an endless number of architectures available when building your OpenStack infrastructure for the first time. The good news is that companies like RedHat and Mirantis are making it easier to fit the storage and cloud layers together. And these are a good starting point for building your OpenStack cloud. But if you want your own OpenStack deployment to be more automated, you can only do this by selecting a storage layer that supports this.
